About Us

Palm house is an inclusive home providing compassionate care to all residents regardless of race, gender, religion, or age.
We provide 24 hour quality care to all those who need our long or short-term services and support.

 

Our family atmosphere welcomes residents, families, and friends.
Palm House is a place where one can enjoy delicious meals cooked daily from scratch, fun games, regular entertainment and special events.

 

Caring excellence begins with caring hearts. At Palm House, we focus on customizing a care plan for each resident.
The management and staff take a personalized care approach in which we care for the whole individual, mind, body and spirit.

Our Mission

Provide exceptional senior care through the engagement of our experienced, dedicated,
and compassionate team leveraging partnerships with the best healthcare professionals
in the Valley, as well as innovative technologies to enhance service efficiency and empower residents to participate in their own healthcare.

 

This fosters dignity, choice, and interdependence with our wonderful residents and families.
